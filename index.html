ASTRONAUT'S JOURNAL

DISCOVER WORLDS WITHIN WORLDS

Astronaut's Journal is a private journalling app for introspection.

Astronaut's Journal consists of six categories of journals, and a 'Discover' section.

The first three journals are for investigating your life as it relates to you, while the last three journals are for investigating your life as it relates to the outside world.

While some of the journals are to be used for everyday, you can use others as you need. Lastly, the Discover section includes stimulating questions that will inspire you to write.

INNER SELF

FREE FLOW JOURNAL

Let your subconscious flow, and then slow.

Free Flow Journal is designed to help you clear your mind by writing two pages without pausing everyday. You will be alerted when you complete two pages.

GOOD THINGS JOURNAL

With Good Things Journal, take a note of the good things in your life that you're grateful for.

DREAM JOURNAL

Bursts of your subconscious during sleep.

With Dream Journal, dive into the symbols of your unconscious mind that leak in your sleep. Notice the recurring themes.

OUTER SELF

IDEAS JOURNAL

Your ideas, and sparks.

Note down your spontaneous ideas about work or snippets for your creative projects. Daily, weekly, or monthly.

GOALS JOURNAL

Keep your short or long term goals here. Plan them out and strategize on how to get results in multiple ways.

ACHIEVEMENTS JOURNAL

The golds you built.

Keep these to remind you in the future for what you are capable of. Note down what worked and what didn't, together with crucial steps that resulted in success.
